Moving Day

Leaving the 8 Million vnd Pad
Oct 28, 2023 4 min read
1000

There have been so many things, how do I begin? I'll do it from the middle as usual. I spent the morning cleaning up, gathering all my possessions and packing them into one medium and and one small suitcase. And as usual, thoughts streamed through my head.

God, please direct me into right thinking. God says, "Keep it simple."

1900

It was a fabulous day. I got my full deposit returned, dropped my bike off at the train station for shipping to HCM, and I made my flight booking to HCM for tomorrow afternoon. I even had time for one last massage at my new favorite place here. I'm now back at The Hive Homestay. It's nice and quiet here. 450,000 a night. A little more than I'd like to pay since I'll be on vacation for at least a few months. I've gotta start paying a monthly rate as soon as possible. 450k vnd a night is too much. I also need to snap some picks.
