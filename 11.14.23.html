The south-East Asian Triangle

Crossroads
Tuesday, Nov 14 2023
1413

Tomorrow makes exactly one week since I've stopped working so I've been busy with life things. Morning gyming, banking, moving rooms, packing, researching travel info and making plans.

Web-programming work has been one of the daily todo's, but not as much as usual. About an hour has been blogging for this site.

I'd like to meet fellow web-programmers. I'm sure they can put in the right stream to make a living doing this, but I can't show them this site. It reads like a diary. There's too much ultra personal information here, especially what I wrote yesterday about my perverted thoughts.

I'm planning on busing it to Phenom Phen on Wednesday. I'll go to the Thai embassy and apply for a tourist visa as soon as I get there, and leave in maybe about a week. I'll trust people and mot of all, I'll trust myself.

All my main writing has been here. I've stopped Google Docsing my writing because it's just writing that no one will ever see, and hey!, I'm a web-programmer. What better way to learn web programming than to work on my own site; especially this one, hosted free on Github pages. I've nailed it!

2045

Silent is Crazy

Im super coool when I speak with people. When I don’t people think I’m crazy. Moral is I have to talk with people and let them know I am a listener and a healer. I should do more of this phone writing. I’ve been spending quality time on my computer writing when it should be done in the evening when I’d normally be out drinking. It’s better done on my phone than at a desk. The guest house I stayed ar in Saigon at five years sober was a cool place. I felt my best in hindsight. I spoke with people and they responded. They treated me with respect and actually enjoyed my company. The same with Emg. My mistake was not trusting myself and getting to know people. When I start working somewhere, I need to accept invitations. Teamwork and getting to know people is a treasured skill and must be cultivated. So that’s mainly what’s been on my mind these past few days. I’ve been walking around with my head up my ass for years. The best years were the ones in recovery when I was honest with myself and others.

God, Please direct me into right thinking.
