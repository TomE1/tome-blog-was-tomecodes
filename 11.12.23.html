Progress, Not Perfection

Does the title fit the message?
Nov 12 2023
0724

Improvements

I spent a few minutes this morning making global improvements to this site and creating templates. The results are awesome - hence the title, "Progress, Not Perfection". One day I'm changing the fonts.

I'm changing homestays again today. The Buddha Homestay was ok, but I'd like a change to broaden my experiences. The neighborhood eateries are galore, but I'll move uptown for a few days. I'll also get laundry done at this new place - a washing machine with roof hang drying is provided.

I'll also pick-up my bike from the train station today. I'd like to take a trip to district 7 to see the old neighborhood but not planning on doing much riding. Maybe I'll go to the other Little Korea in Saigon to check it out. I'm looking for cheap massage places.

Last Night

I had a massage last night in Bui Vien. I ventured out with 400 and a bit of change - not much but I always return empty pocketed so there's no point taking a lot of money unless I intend on losing it all.

I walked past a massage place and the woman said there's a special rate of 200 for an hour. I checked my pockets and counted 240 - that's all I had. She said it was enough so I had a massage. Normally they ask for a few hundred in tip but this woman didn't mind taking what I had.

I spent the other half of my money earlier at a hostess bar. 190 for a lady drink and 40k for a bottle of soda water.

I bought a bottle of water at a convenience store and returned home broke.

I thought about my current situation and the situation in Saigon. I decided to stay here until my visa expires in a few weeks.

Saigon's Happening

There's the sleazy side and very few tourists which makes this a good place for me to work, program, write and design. The food is fabulous and the people are awesome.The cost of living is quite low. It's so much better than living and working in Bangkok. So, that's my plan. I'll look for work here.

Hanoi

As for Hanoi, I've grown less fond of the place. I never gave it a chance but I find the locals to be cold and impossible to get to know. The job and money was good but I didn't have a choice to stay so I'm gone. I found a good masseuse, gym and a few places to work but I've spent long enough there. It was good while it lasted. It really was time for me to move on.

God, Please direct me into right thinking.

0900

I feel I'm learning so much more by working on my own projects. Watching tutorials and coding along has had it's merits but it's limited. It's time for me to start creating my own projects with what I've learnt.

Tom Evans

I surrender to God everyday. It's the best I can do.
