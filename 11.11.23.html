First Day in Saigon

New Frontiers
Nov 11, 2023 4 min read
1830

I was getting my haircut this morning when I was triggered by the barber. He said I looked sad and told me to tell him about it. I got annoyed and told him I'll pay and leave.

There were so many things running through my mind. I'm not working now so my thoughts are running wild. I went to a coffee shop and wrote a list of all that was on my mind. There was a lot. Of course I'd be irritated by the barber's questions, but I need to control my anger. I'm spending too much time thinking and not enough time doing.

I went back to my pad and did some unpaid work on my computer. I fell asleep for the second time today which isn't good. I get so much more done when I'm at a cafe.

God, Please direct me into right thinking.

--

Tom Evans

I surrender to God everyday. It's the best I can do.
