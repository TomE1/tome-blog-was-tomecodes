The Crossroads

Vietnam or Cambodia
Sunday, Nov 19 2023
0946

I'm sending out my cv tomorrow. I'm a 57 year old teacher and I'm looking for a teaching position in Cambodia. I was considering Saigon but I thought Cambodia would be better. It's a new experience and the people may be a bit warmer.

My motorbike is in Saigon so I could quickly transition into a new teaching position there. Should I send my CV out and see what offers I get? Perhaps I should start a pros and cons list to clarify my thoughts.

On a side note, yesterday I pointed my website to my new domain. Now I'm findable on the internet with a custom URL. That's forward progress.

And yesterday I caught a small code. It's probably because of the lack of hygiene here. The notes are dirty and I was touching my eye. I'll fight through this.

I'm moving to the Russian Market area tomorrow. I think it's a bit cleaner and it's near my fitness center and local eateries.

I'm lucky to have options. I'll continue my 2 hours of studying coding.

God, Please direct me into right thinking.
